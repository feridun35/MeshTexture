Free Online 3D Mesh Texturing Tool

Mesh Texture Pro is a browser-based application that lets you apply procedural 3D textures directly onto STL mesh files — no installation required.

How to apply 3D textures to STL files?

  1. Load your STL file — Import any standard binary or ASCII STL model into the viewport.
  2. Select surfaces & choose a texture — Click the faces you want to emboss, then pick a preset texture (carbon fiber, hexagon, knurling…) or upload your own JPG/PNG.
  3. Bake & export — Adjust scale, amplitude, and rotation, then hit Apply (Bake) to displace the geometry and download the modified STL file ready for 3D printing.
